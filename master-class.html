Rose

The rose is a symbol of love and passion. Different colors of roses convey various meanings, such as red roses symbolizing love and desire, while white roses represent purity and innocence.

Tulip

Tulips are associated with perfect love. Red tulips symbolize true love, while yellow tulips represent cheerful thoughts and sunshine.

Peonies

Peonies are a symbol of prosperity and good fortune. They also represent romance and are often used in weddings to symbolize a happy marriage.

Sunflower

Sunflowers symbolize adoration, loyalty, and longevity. They are known for their vibrant appearance and their association with warmth and happiness.
